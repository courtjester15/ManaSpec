EASY / EARLY SIGNALS (build these first)

These are high signal, low complexity, very trackable:

1. Price Movement Signals
% change (1D / 7D)
gap up / gap down
new local high / low

👉 baseline signal engine

2. Volume / Activity Spike
volume > X day average
unusual activity flag
“attention spike”

👉 this replaces a lot of weak “seller list” value

3. Portfolio Movement Signals
biggest position gains/losses
contribution to P&L (simple version)
“what is affecting me today”
4. Target State Changes
target hit (up/down)
target nearly hit (within % threshold)
stop triggered

👉 this becomes your “truth events layer”

5. New Entry / New Activity
asset becomes active in radar for first time
first spike detected after dormancy

👉 super easy, very powerful early signal

🟡 NEXT STEP SIGNALS (still v1, but later)
6. Trend State Flip
uptrend → neutral
neutral → downtrend
simple moving average crossover logic
7. Volatility Expansion / Contraction
range compression
sudden expansion
“something is about to happen” indicator
8. Multi-Day Strength / Weakness
consistent gain streak
consistent decline streak
